About

My name is Eric Schneider (he/him). I was born in Charlotte, North Carolina, and raised near there. I am currently attending the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill as a sophmore.

I am working to obtain a Bachelor's of Science in the majors of computer science and a mathematics. In computer science, I am currently mainly taking prerequisites. After those are done (should be roughly after fall), I plan to focus on classes that are required preparation for the Master of Science (see "Background Preparation," here). In Mathematics, I'm mainly taking classes that are requirements for both my majors. I plan to take classes that are relevant to computer science and scientific computation after that.

Computer science is the focus on my studies and what I hope to get a career in. I first started programming when I was 12 by copying online YouTube tutorials. Despite having been copied word-for-word, my first program did not work. Nevertheless I persisted, and eventually was good enough at programming to work on things that could be used by others. Nuclear Control 2, a port of a Minecraft mod I was the project leader and main programmer for, has over 4 million downloads. Although the mod is discontinued, I still get money from downloads, even if not much. The Official FTB Wiki, which I am an administrator and major contributor towards (with over 50,000 edits), has effectively become the biggest, most popular and most up-to-date centralized repostiory of knowledge on modded Minecraft. I do not get money from it, although I did receive a job offer which I unfortunately had to turn down. For other projects I've worked on, see my projects page or my resume.

Java is the programming language I feel most natural in. Groovy comes close, but only since they are quite similar. I'm not as skilled in Ruby, but I've always had a thing for it. Its biggest weakness for me is its lack of a decent, free IDE. I still use it to whip up a script when needed. I am also quite skilled in the usage of MediaWiki.

I am openly bisexual.

In in my free time, I like to run and lift weights. I'm not very fast, but I can run long distances. I've run three marathons, which I am very proud of. I like to play video games. Minecraft is my favorite, especially when modded. League of Legends is a favorite too, although I haven't played it as much recently. Other games I enjoy include Cube 2: Sauerbraten, The Madness of Little Emma, the Civilization series, and the Bloons Tower Defense series. Other than gaming, I like to keep up with the news and with international politics. I like cats, including the two my parents own.
